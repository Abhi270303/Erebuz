## Description
A fake "Agni App" mobile investment scam defrauded victims of approximately ₹1 crore (~$120K USD) in India (February 2026). The scam was operated by Sangam Kumar, who was arrested by authorities. While this scam is completely unrelated to the Agni Finance DeFi protocol, it creates:

1. Brand confusion — users searching "Agni" may find scam reports and incorrectly associate them with Agni Finance
2. Reputational risk — the scam may deter users who cannot distinguish the fake app from the legitimate protocol
3. SEO confusion — scam-related articles may rank for "Agni" keywords

## Assessment
The scam is unrelated and the mastermind was arrested. The primary risk is brand confusion rather than direct financial impact to Agni Finance users.

## Recommendation
- Monitor for brand confusion via Google Alerts and social listening
- Consider a blog post clarifying the distinction
- Ensure official dapp URLs are verified on Twitter/X, Discord, and Telegram
- Add prominent warnings on the website if users report confusion
