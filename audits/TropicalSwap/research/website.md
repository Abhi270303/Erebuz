## Tokenomics
- **$PAPPLE:** Native token, max supply 250,000, ERC-20, paired with USDC
- **SEEDS:** Escrowed PAPPLE, non-transferable (except whitelisted), minted 1:1 by locking PAPPLE
- SEEDS can be converted back to PAPPLE via vesting (15 days to 6 months)
- 50% malus on minimum vesting period (burned)
- 0.10% swap fee → LP providers
- 0.10% swap fee → treasury
- 0.05% swap fee → SEEDS holders as Real Yield
